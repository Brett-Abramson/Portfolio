About Me

I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area and in 2014 I moved to Austin, Texas. This was an unintentional destination as I had my eyes set on North Carolina. A simliar environment to the one I grew up loving back home. Lots of trees, mountains, and the ocean isn't too far away. A long story short, car trouble, leading to a lack of funds, meant that I wasn't going anywhere for ahwile while I earned some money. Then, as these things happen, I never made it to North Carolina. I am still happily living in Austin.

Before all that I grew up working in a family restaurant on Fisherman's Wharf in San Francisco. I graduated from San Fransico State University with a degree in Liberal Studies and an emphasis in Multi-displinary Ethnic Studies. The past 8 years I have been a bartender. I am currently a software engineering student at General Assembly. When I am not clacking away at a keyboard, you can find me playing music, brewing beer, or finding something to do outside.
